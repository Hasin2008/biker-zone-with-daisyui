Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most important things I should know about riding a bike?

Safety First! Always obey the rules of the road. Obey all traffic signals, signs, and laws. Get in the mindset of “driving” your bike—not just “riding” your bike. This will help you be a more focused bike rider.

How can I tell if my helmet is old and I need a new one?

Motorcycle helmets have an expiration date, although they take substantially longer to expire. The expiration date is specific to the EPS (expanded polystyrene) layer of the helmet.

My bike has been in storage is it safe to ride?

If your motorcycle has been sitting for years, you should check the fluids, change the oil, check the brakes and tires, inspect the chain and sprocket, and clean the bike before taking it for a test ride.

What rules should I follow when riding my bike?

Follow the law, make your intentions clear to everyone on the road, Be conspicuous, think ahead.
